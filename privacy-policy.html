Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GrooVia, a sole proprietorship registered under MSME (Udyam Registration No. UDYAM-GJ-01-0655980), collects, uses, and protects information when you use groovia.co.in, our WhatsApp-based ordering service, or interact with a shop using GrooVia ("the Service").

1. Who We Are

GrooVia is a WhatsApp-integrated order management tool that helps local kirana (neighbourhood grocery) shops receive and manage customer orders. GrooVia is not a delivery service, does not process payments, and does not operate as a marketplace — each shop listed on GrooVia is an independent, separately owned business.

Business location: Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India
Contact: admin@groovia.co.in

2. Information We Collect

From customers placing an order:

  • Name
  • Phone number (WhatsApp number)
  • Order details (items, quantities, pickup time, payment method selected)

From shopkeepers using the Service:

  • Shop name, owner phone number, shop address
  • Product catalog data (names, prices, stock levels)
  • Order history and status updates

Automatically:

  • Basic technical data when you visit groovia.co.in (browser type, general usage), for keeping the site working correctly. GrooVia does not use tracking cookies for advertising.

We do not collect payment card details, UPI PINs, or bank information — all payments (cash or UPI) are handled directly between the customer and the shop, outside the Service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information above only to:

  • Create and route your order to the correct shop
  • Send order status updates via WhatsApp (accepted, rejected, ready, completed)
  • Let shopkeepers manage their catalog, stock, and daily orders
  • Maintain a basic order history for the customer and shop
  • Improve the reliability of the Service

We do not sell your personal data to third parties, and we do not use your order or chat content for advertising.

4. WhatsApp & Meta

GrooVia sends and receives order-related messages using the Meta WhatsApp Business Platform (Cloud API). When you place an order or interact with a shop via WhatsApp, your message content is transmitted through Meta's infrastructure to deliver the service. Meta acts as a data processor for this messaging traffic under its own Business Messaging data terms. Meta does not use this traffic to serve you ads.

By placing an order or messaging a shop through GrooVia on WhatsApp, you consent to receive service-related messages (order confirmations, status updates) via WhatsApp for that order.

5. Data Storage & Retention

Order and account data is stored on secured cloud infrastructure (Supabase/PostgreSQL) with encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS). We retain order history for as long as reasonably needed to provide the Service and meet basic business record-keeping needs, after which it may be anonymized or deleted.

6. Your Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and applicable Indian law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal/record-keeping requirements
  • Withdraw consent for future messages (you can stop an order conversation at any time)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at admin@groovia.co.in.

Grievance Officer: Anushka Shah, reachable at admin@groovia.co.in / +91 88664 16442.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures (HTTPS, access controls) to protect your data. No system is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to safeguard the information you share with us.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date on this page.

11. Data Deletion and Your Rights

We believe in your right to control your data. At any time, you may request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data. To submit a request, please visit our Data Deletion Request Page or email us at admin@groovia.co.in.

Upon receipt of your request, we will verify your identity and process the deletion within 30 days. Please note that certain data may be retained for legally mandated periods (such as tax invoices or financial records) as required by Indian law.
